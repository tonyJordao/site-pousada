A POUSADA

Located in Baía do Sueste, 200 meters from the beach, sustainability-awarded Pousada Maravilha was thought to frame in memory the paradisiacal scenery and the turquoise tones of the bay. Its refinement and exclusivity can be seen in each space that combines comfort and rusticity in an environment totally integrated with nature. From the endless edge pool, the large balconies of the luxury apartments and bungalows, the restaurant lounge, among the best on the island, the wonder of paradise will be at your fingertips and will eternalize the moments lived during your stay.
